  7. What is the importance of universal law of gravitation?
    8. Importance of universal law of gravitation is:
    (i)It is the gravitational force that binds us to the earth.
    (ii) It is gravitational force which provides circular motion of the Moon around the Earth;
    (iii)Its the gravitational force which provides the necessary centripetal force required for
    circular motion of planets around the Sun; and
    (iv)It is the gravitational force on Earth due to Moon and Sun that provides tides.
    In general it is the gravitational force which binds up our solar system and stars of galaxies
    beyond our solar system.



  8. What is the acceleration of free fall?

    acting on it. Due to this force the body experiences an acceleration towards centre of Earth.This
    acceleration is the acceleration of free fall and is called the acceleration due to gravity.
